Praxis

  • Praxis is Metamation next generation smart-factory solution for sheet metal. Praxis combines the intelligence of the Flux engine with the capabilities of MetaCAM to offer a seamless solution:
  • Flux is Metamation next generation software for programming press-brakes and panel benders. It offers the highest level of intelligence and automation for computing bend sequences and tool setups.
  • MetaCAM is Metamation CAM system for punch, laser and combination machines. With support for vast number of sheet metal machines, MetaCAM has an established track record of providing a high level of support for advanced machine capabilities.
